The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

10
5/5
Adventure Nintendo Switch

A Masterclass in Open-World Design

Nintendo expands upon Breath of the Wild's foundation with even more creativity and freedom.

Tears of the Kingdom takes the already incredible world of Hyrule and adds new layers—literally—with sky islands and underground caverns. The new abilities, like Ultrahand and Fuse, allow for unprecedented creativity in solving puzzles and combat.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game introduces new mechanics that encourage experimentation. Building vehicles, crafting weapons, and exploring vertically designed environments make this one of the most immersive Zelda experiences yet.

Pros

  • Unmatched freedom and creativity
  • Massive, layered world to explore
  • Engaging story and characters
  • Perfectly refined gameplay

Cons

  • Performance dips in busy areas
  • Some may find the learning curve steep

Final Verdict

Tears of the Kingdom is a monumental achievement in game design, offering endless possibilities and a world that feels truly alive.
