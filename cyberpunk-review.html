The Redemption of Night City

After a rocky launch, Cyberpunk 2077 has evolved into the immersive RPG experience we were promised.

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game offers three distinct character classes - Nomad, Street Kid, and Corpo - each with unique starting points that affect dialogue options and some missions. The skill tree is expansive, allowing for deep customization of your playstyle.

Pros

  • Stunning visuals and art direction
  • Engaging main story and characters
  • Deep character customization
  • Improved performance after updates

Cons

  • Some minor bugs remain
  • Police system could be better
  • Driving mechanics feel floaty

Final Verdict

Cyberpunk 2077 has redeemed itself with extensive updates and the excellent Phantom Liberty expansion. What was once a disappointment is now one of the most immersive RPGs available.
