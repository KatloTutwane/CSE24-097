Resident Evil 4 Remake

9.5
4.5/5
Survival Horror PC PS5 Xbox

A Masterful Reimagining

Capcom delivers one of the best remakes in gaming history, revitalizing a classic for modern audiences.

Resident Evil 4 Remake reimagines the 2005 classic with modern graphics, updated controls, and expanded story elements while maintaining the tense atmosphere and action-horror balance that made the original so beloved.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game features over-the-shoulder shooting with improved movement and aiming mechanics. The parry system adds depth to combat, and the knife now has durability. Inventory management remains strategic with the iconic attache case system.

Pros

  • Faithful yet innovative remake
  • Improved combat mechanics
  • Stunning visuals and sound design
  • Expanded story content

Cons

  • Some cut content from original
  • Occasional AI issues
  • Mercenaries mode launched later

Final Verdict

Resident Evil 4 Remake sets a new standard for game remakes, delivering a fresh experience that honors the original while improving nearly every aspect.
