A Dark Return to Form

Blizzard returns to Diablo's darker roots with a satisfying, if familiar, action RPG experience.

Diablo IV is an action RPG that combines the series' signature loot-driven gameplay with a darker, more gothic aesthetic reminiscent of Diablo II. Players choose from five classes and battle through Sanctuary to stop Lilith's return.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game features an open world with shared zones, deep character customization through skill trees and Paragon boards, and satisfying combat. The always-online requirement and live-service elements are controversial but the core gameplay loop remains addictive.

Pros

  • Excellent combat feel
  • Beautiful dark aesthetic
  • Deep character customization
  • Strong campaign story

Cons

  • Always-online requirement
  • Repetitive endgame
  • Monetization concerns

Final Verdict

Diablo IV delivers a satisfying core experience with excellent combat and atmosphere, though its live-service elements may frustrate some players.
