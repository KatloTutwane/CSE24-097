God of War: Ragnarök

9.8
5/5
Action PS5 PS4

A Masterful Conclusion to the Norse Saga

Santa Monica Studio delivers an epic, emotional, and visually stunning sequel that surpasses its predecessor.

God of War: Ragnarök continues Kratos and Atreus' journey through the Nine Realms as they face the impending doom of Ragnarök. With breathtaking visuals, refined combat, and a deeply emotional story, this sequel is a masterpiece.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The combat system has been expanded with new weapons, abilities, and enemy types. Atreus plays a more significant role, and the puzzle-solving elements are more intricate. The game also introduces new realms to explore, each with unique environments and challenges.

Pros

  • Emotionally gripping narrative
  • Improved combat and exploration
  • Stunning visuals and sound design
  • Excellent character development

Cons

  • Some pacing issues in the middle
  • Minor camera frustrations in combat

Final Verdict

God of War: Ragnarök is a near-perfect action-adventure game that delivers on every front. It's a must-play for fans of the series and newcomers alike.
