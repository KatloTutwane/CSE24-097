Final Fantasy XVI

9.0
4/5
Action RPG PS5

A Bold New Direction

Final Fantasy XVI embraces full action combat while delivering one of the series' strongest narratives.

Final Fantasy XVI is an action RPG that tells the story of Clive Rosfield across three stages of his life. The game features real-time combat, massive Eikon battles, and a mature, political storyline inspired by Game of Thrones.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game completely abandons turn-based combat for a Devil May Cry-inspired action system. Players can equip abilities from different Eikons and chain together combos. The game features spectacular set-piece battles between massive summons.

Pros

  • Excellent story and characters
  • Spectacular Eikon battles
  • Great voice acting
  • Beautiful world design

Cons

  • Lack of RPG depth
  • Weak side content
  • Performance mode issues

Final Verdict

Final Fantasy XVI is a bold reinvention that delivers one of the series' best stories and most spectacular set pieces, even if its RPG elements feel light.
