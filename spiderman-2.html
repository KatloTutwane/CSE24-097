Marvel's Spider-Man 2

9.3
4.5/5
Action-Adventure PS5

Bigger, Better, and More Spectacular

Insomniac delivers a worthy sequel that improves upon its predecessors in nearly every way.

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 is an action-adventure game that continues the stories of Peter Parker and Miles Morales as they face new threats including Venom and Kraven the Hunter. The game features an expanded New York City with Brooklyn and Queens.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game introduces web wings for faster traversal, symbiote powers for Peter, and refined combat mechanics. Players can switch between Peter and Miles seamlessly throughout the story. The expanded city offers more variety in activities and side content.

Pros

  • Amazing traversal mechanics
  • Engaging dual protagonist story
  • Beautiful visuals and performance
  • Improved combat variety

Cons

  • Some repetitive side activities
  • Pacing issues in third act
  • Wanted more Venom gameplay

Final Verdict

Spider-Man 2 is a spectacular sequel that refines everything that worked in the first game while introducing meaningful improvements and an emotionally compelling story.
