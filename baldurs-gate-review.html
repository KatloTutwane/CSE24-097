Baldur's Gate 3

9.7
5/5
CRPG PC PS5 Xbox

The New Gold Standard for CRPGs

Larian Studios has created a masterpiece that brings Dungeons & Dragons to life like never before.

Baldur's Gate 3 is a CRPG that faithfully adapts the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ruleset into a rich, narrative-driven experience. With deep character customization, meaningful choices, and turn-based tactical combat, it sets a new benchmark for the genre.

Gameplay and Mechanics

The game features an incredibly detailed world where nearly every action has consequences. The turn-based combat is tactical and rewarding, while the narrative offers unparalleled player freedom and reactivity.

Pros

  • Unparalleled player freedom and choice
  • Deep, tactical combat system
  • Exceptional writing and voice acting
  • Beautifully realized D&D world

Cons

  • Steep learning curve for newcomers
  • Some bugs and performance issues
  • Very long playtime (100+ hours)

Final Verdict

Baldur's Gate 3 is a monumental achievement in RPG design, offering an unprecedented level of player freedom and narrative depth that will be remembered for years to come.
